White Noise experiment

Step 1

Press '1' on your keyboard to play repeated noise.

Press 'Escape' once you have heard the repeat a few times.

You're welcome to replay the noise as many times as you like.

Step 2

Next, try to locate the repeat that you heard in the noise by filtering through time and frequency.

You can do this by moving and modifying the shape of the grey rectangle on the canvas below.

You can filter higher frequencies by moving the rectangle upwards, and listen to segments further in time by moving the box to the right.

Move the position of the rectangle with the following arrows ↑↓←→

Increase the height of the rectangle using 'h'.

Decrease the height of the rectangle using 'shift' and 'h'.

Increase the width of the rectangle using 'w'.

Decrease the width of the rectangle using 'shift' and 'w'.
